## 2. CHARACTERS (12)

### Ironveil Syndicate NPCs

#### Kira Voss - Contract Enforcer
- **Role:** Debt collector, corporate enforcer
- **Personality:** Cold, calculating, respects efficiency
- **Quote:** "Every credit spent is an investment. Every debt collected is a return."
- **Visual Description:** Professional but intimidating, corporate attire with military edge, calculating eyes, data tablet or contract hologram

#### Director Chen - Mining Operations Director
- **Role:** Executive, operations manager
- **Personality:** Impatient, bottom-line focused, secretly respects boldness
- **Quote:** "I don't pay for explanations. I pay for results."
- **Visual Description:** Executive suit, older, stern expression, surrounded by mining operation holograms, conveying wealth and power

### Ashfall Remnants NPCs

#### Jax "Redhawk" Mora - Raid Captain
- **Role:** Raid leader, freedom fighter
- **Personality:** Passionate, protective of refugees, distrusts authority
- **Quote:** "The corps call us pirates. We call it wealth redistribution."
- **Visual Description:** Rugged, scarred, flight suit patched together, red hawk insignia, fierce determination, rebel leader aesthetic

#### Elder Nomi - Refugee Council Speaker
- **Role:** Council leader, historian, survivor
- **Personality:** Wise, weary, remembers what was lost in the Collapse
- **Quote:** "We remember the stations that burned. We will not let that happen again."
- **Visual Description:** Elderly, dignified despite worn clothing, carries weight of lost history, traditional garments from lost colony

### Meridian Accord NPCs

#### Soren Vale - Licensed Broker
- **Role:** Information dealer, middleman
- **Personality:** Charming, calculating angles, truly neutral
- **Quote:** "Information is currency. I deal in both."
- **Visual Description:** Smooth, well-dressed, theatrical gestures, multiple screens showing data, mask-like pleasant expression

#### Magistrate Yun - Arbitration Court
- **Role:** Judge, arbiter of disputes
- **Personality:** Fair, formal, believes deeply in process
- **Quote:** "All disputes can be resolved. The question is: at what price?"
- **Visual Description:** Formal judicial robes, balanced scales imagery, neutral expression, courtroom setting

### Void Wardens NPCs

#### Captain Thresh - Patrol Commander
- **Role:** Military commander, patrol leader
- **Personality:** Stoic, honorable, follows old codes without question
- **Quote:** "The void has laws older than any faction. We enforce them."
- **Visual Description:** Military bearing, ancient ceremonial armor mixed with practical gear, stern but noble, veteran presence

#### Keeper Ash - Beacon Master
- **Role:** Navigator, mystic, beacon maintainer
- **Personality:** Mysterious, speaks in riddles, sees patterns others miss
- **Quote:** "The beacons remember. Listen, and they will guide you."
- **Visual Description:** Hooded, surrounded by beacon light, star charts, slightly ethereal, seeing beyond normal sight

### Sundered Oath NPCs

#### "Ghost" - Information Dealer
- **Role:** Black market information broker
- **Personality:** Paranoid, opportunistic, genuinely helpful if paid
- **Quote:** "Names are chains. I cut mine loose long ago."
- **Visual Description:** Face obscured, hooded, in shadows, multiple identities suggested, anonymous but competent

#### Razor - Mercenary Captain
- **Role:** Mercenary, gun for hire
- **Personality:** Direct, violent, surprisingly honorable about contracts
- **Quote:** "I don't do loyalty. But I do finish jobs."
- **Visual Description:** Battle-scarred, visible weapons, no faction insignia, dangerous professionalism, stripped-down combat gear

### Player Crew

#### Chief Engineer Kai - Ship Systems

## 5. LOCATIONS (9)

### Stellaris Dawn
- **Type:** Derelict Colony Ship
- **Description:** A massive pre-Collapse colony vessel drifting in contested space. Contains 47 colonists in cryogenic suspension. Multiple factions claim salvage rights.
- **Visual Elements:** Vast ship hull, damage from drift, cryopod chambers, faded colony insignia, contested territory markers

### Shattered Reach
- **Type:** Player Base Region
- **Description:** A lawless region on the edge of faction space, filled with ancient ruins and debris fields. Home to opportunists and those seeking to avoid faction oversight.
- **Visual Elements:** Asteroid debris, ancient wreckage, hidden bases, frontier outpost aesthetic, dangerous beauty

### The Void / The Black
- **Type:** Deep Space
- **Description:** The vast emptiness between faction territories. Dangerous, lawless, where the Void Wardens patrol.
- **Visual Elements:** Starfield, isolation, distant nebulae, beacon lights in darkness, vast and threatening

### Debris Field / Graveyard
- **Type:** Battle Site
- **Description:** Site of an ancient battle containing the wreck of the Oathkeeper, a pre-Collapse vessel with dangerous technology.
- **Visual Elements:** Ship wreckage, floating debris, ancient battle damage, hidden treasure, dangerous salvage

### The Oathkeeper (Wreck)
- **Type:** Pre-Collapse Vessel
- **Description:** Ancient ship containing pre-Collapse weapons technology. Object of faction conflict.
- **Visual Elements:** Ancient design, advanced technology, battle damage, mysterious power signatures

### Contested Zone
- **Type:** Disputed Territory
- **Description:** Areas where multiple factions claim overlapping jurisdiction. Tense, frequent skirmishes.
- **Visual Elements:** Multiple faction markers, patrol ships, tension, boundary lines

### Station Seven (Fallen)
- **Type:** Destroyed Industrial Station
- **Description:** Former Ironveil foundry, now destroyed. Birthplace of the Ironclad. Historical significance.
- **Visual Elements:** Ruined station, foundry equipment, industrial decay, historical weight

### Sanctuary Station
- **Type:** Neutral Port
- **Description:** Refugee destination in unclaimed space. Hope for the displaced.
- **Visual Elements:** Crowded, diverse ships, hope amid hardship, neutral flags, haven

### Outer Reaches / Void Warden Territory
- **Type:** Patrol Zone
- **Description:** The far edges of known space where Void Wardens maintain ancient beacons and enforce old laws.
- **Visual Elements:** Beacon towers, patrol routes, ancient stations, lonely vigilance

## 6. QUEST VISUAL THEMES (3)

### Quest 1: The Salvage Claim (Ironveil)
- **Narrative:** Corporate salvage operation of colony ship with refugee survivors
- **Visual Tone:** Industrial, corporate, cold efficiency vs. human cost
- **Key Imagery:** Mining equipment, corporate logos, cryopods, contract documents, industrial yellow

### Quest 2: The Sanctuary Run (Ashfall)
- **Narrative:** Guide refugee convoy through military blockade to safety
- **Visual Tone:** Desperate hope, makeshift survival, warmth against cold space
- **Key Imagery:** Crowded ships, families, blockade runners, orange/fire motifs, convoy formation

### Quest 3: The Broker's Gambit (Meridian)
- **Narrative:** Mediate faction dispute over pre-Collapse technology before war erupts
- **Visual Tone:** Political intrigue, hidden agendas, ancient secrets
- **Key Imagery:** Diplomatic chambers, hidden weapons, ancient tech, blue neutrality, masks and deception

## Art Style Notes

Based on the game's tone and themes:

- **Overall Aesthetic:** Gritty sci-fi, lived-in universe, faction conflicts
- **Ship Design:** Functional over elegant, faction-distinctive silhouettes
- **Character Art:** Portraits with personality, faction-appropriate attire
- **Color Usage:** Strong faction color identity, desaturated backgrounds
- **Mood:** Morally gray, survival themes, political tension
